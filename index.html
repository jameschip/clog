What?

A clog is a tiny blog that is read in the terminal using curl (curl + blog = clog).
You can read one by adding one of the addresses above to the end of the command "curl -s"

Can I have a clog?

YES! Follw the instructions here!

Can I get my clog listed here?

You can.
Your clog must reference back to being a member of this clogring in your index header.
No clogs that contain racist, sexist, ableist or otherwise discriminatory or illegal content will be accepted.
You can add you page by putting it in the list of pages ^up there^ and opening a pull request!
